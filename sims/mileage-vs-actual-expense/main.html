Key Notes
  • 2025 IRS standard mileage rate: $0.70/mile
  • You must choose your method in the first year you use the vehicle for business. Standard → Actual is generally allowed; Actual → Standard is NOT allowed for the same vehicle.
  • Keep a mileage log regardless of method (IRS requires contemporaneous records).
  • Commuting to a regular workplace is never deductible.
  • Section 179 and MACRS deductions are subject to luxury auto limits even if business use > 50%.
