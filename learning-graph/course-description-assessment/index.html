    Gap Analysis¶

    1. Target Audience (−1 pt)¶

    The audience is well-described but does not use a formal education-level classification. The skill framework expects a category such as "adult continuing education" or "general public / all levels." This is cosmetic but useful for learning graph calibration.

    2. Learning Outcomes Header (−1 pt)¶

    The Bloom's Taxonomy section is titled "Learning Objectives by Bloom's Taxonomy" but is missing the standard lead-in statement: "After this course, students will be able to…" This phrase helps the learning graph generator anchor all concepts to learner outcomes.

    3. Remember Level (−2 pts)¶

    Only 3 outcomes present. Missing outcomes that would unlock additional concept nodes:

    • Recall IRS record retention requirements (3 years, 6 years, 7 years rules)
    • Recognize the difference between a W-2 and a 1099
    • List the types of income that are NOT taxable (gifts, inheritances, some benefits)

    4. Understand Level (−2 pts)¶

    Only 3 outcomes. Missing outcomes:

    • Explain the difference between a tax refund and actually owing less tax
    • Describe what happens when you file late or miss a payment
    • Explain what adjusted gross income (AGI) is and why it matters

    5. Analyze Level (−2 pts)¶

    One outcome uses the verb "Evaluate" (a higher Bloom's level) instead of an Analyze-level verb. This creates a taxonomy overlap. Also needs 4+ outcomes:

    • Needs fix: "Evaluate which tax-advantaged accounts best match your situation" → reword to "Compare tax-advantaged account options based on income and tax situation"
    • Missing: "Distinguish between legitimate business deductions and personal expenses"
    • Missing: "Break down the components of a tax bill (income tax vs SE tax vs FICA)"

    6. Evaluate Level (−1 pt)¶

    Strong but could add:

    • "Evaluate whether a given advanced tax strategy (Augusta Rule, backdoor Roth) applies to your financial situation"

    7. Create Level (−2 pts)¶

    3 outcomes are present but no capstone project is described. Suggested capstone:

    • Missing: "Produce a complete personal tax blueprint: a mock completed 1040, annotated W-2/1099, a quarterly estimated tax schedule, and a business deduction log"

    Improvement Suggestions (Prioritized)¶

    Priority 1 — Add capstone to Create level¶

    A capstone project description significantly improves learning graph richness. Suggested addition:

    Create a complete personal tax blueprint: a mock completed 1040, an annotated W-2/1099 pair, a quarterly estimated tax schedule, and a business deduction log for one year of side hustle income.

    Priority 2 — Fix the Analyze/Evaluate taxonomy slip¶

    Move "Evaluate which tax-advantaged accounts best match your situation" from Analyze to Evaluate, and replace it with a true analysis outcome using verbs like: distinguish, differentiate, compare, break down, categorize, examine.

    Priority 3 — Add 2 more outcomes to Remember and Understand¶

    These unlock more foundational concept nodes in the learning graph. Use verbs: recall, recognize, list, name, define (Remember) and explain, describe, summarize, classify (Understand).

    Priority 4 — Add formal audience level classification¶

    Add one line: **Educational level:** Adult continuing education / general public
