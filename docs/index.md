# Tax Return Guide (2025)

**A plain-English guide to filing taxes, saving money legally, and understanding the US tax system.**

---

## What This Book Is

Most tax guides are either too vague to be useful or written for accountants. This book is different.

In plain English — with real dollar amounts, real examples, and zero jargon — you will learn how to:

- **File your own taxes** without paying someone hundreds of dollars
- **Legally reduce what you owe** using strategies most people miss
- **Understand every form** the IRS sends you
- **Handle side hustle and self-employment income** without getting blindsided in April
- **Plan year-round** so tax season is never a surprise again

Whether you are filing for the first time, dealing with 1099 income, or trying to stop leaving money on the table — this guide is for you.

## How to Use This Book

- **Complete beginner?** Start with Chapter 1.
- **Need to file right now?** Jump to Chapter 2.
- **Have a side hustle?** Start with Chapter 4.
- **Want to pay less in taxes?** Start with Chapter 3, then go to Chapter 9.

!!! tip "This book uses 2025 tax law"
    All brackets, limits, and rules reflect the 2025 tax year (filed in early 2026). Rules change annually — always verify current limits at [IRS.gov](https://irs.gov).
